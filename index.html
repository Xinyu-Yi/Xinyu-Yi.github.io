Xinyu Yi

I'm currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student at Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Feng Xu. Before this, I obtained my B.S degree in Computer Science from School of the Gifted Young, University of Science and Technology of China in 2020.

I'm interested in human motion capture and physics-based human-environement interaction. I'm currently working on real-time human motion capture from sparse lightweight body-worn sensors.
